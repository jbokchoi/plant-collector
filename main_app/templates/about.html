About the Plant Minder

Taking care of your plants can be hard.

Say good-bye to dead plants, and hello to healthy, happy plants.

With so many different types of plants out there it's hard to keep track of which plant needs lots of attention versus the ones that are happy left alone.

Using Plant Minder, keep track of all of your leafy friends - monitor who needs lots of sunshine, little water, etc.
